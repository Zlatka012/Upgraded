Breakdown of the elevator mechanism:

Elevator can be operated from the inside by the control panel in the cabin or by the buttons located on every floor from the outside of the elevator. (Note that ground floor has number 1.)

When the elevator is waiting for passengers to enter the cabin or is moving towards/with currect user, no other person can call it at the same time. That is indicated by lights on every floor - they can't push the "call" button while they're lighten up. (Surely they can, but nothing will happen.)

If the elevator stands still, anyone from outside can operate it. If it's on the same floor then the door will open. If not, elevator will come directly to caller and open door automatically. Then, the passenger has 3 seconds to enter desired floor. If no one enters the cabin and dial his choice, door will automatically close themselves and elevator will be ready to be called from any floor.

Usage instructions:

1. Click on the floor button you're calling from (- on the right side when using desktop, at the bottom while being on the phone).
2. When elevator arrives at your floor and opens its door, select the desired destination in the inside of the cabin.
3. When doors are opened, you can operate it from inside as many times as you want.
4. When calling buttons aren't lit up, you can call it by using them.
